It is with great sadness that we inform you of the passing of
our beloved President of the Japan Branch, Tokiko Shigure.

She had been a valued member of our organization since the
early days of its inception. She continually upheld the beliefs
of Naixatloz and relentlessly fought for a better future for
everyone in this world.
